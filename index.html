Helpful Staff

We know that it might be difficult to find the right school, college or university and apply to become an international student. Our professional staff is highly trained in F-1 processes and is here to help you from start to finish. We will assist in filling out your application to scheduling classes.

Programs Variety

We offer a variety of programs to chose from. Whether you are looking to learn English, launch a new career or advance your education and earn your first Master's degree, we will help you find the right program for you.

Affordability

International students must show their ability to sustain tuition and living expenses while in the U.S., but there are many more options. We will assess your individual situation and help you find the most suitable program with affordable tuition and/or scholarship options.

Opportunity for Professional
Experience and Employment in the U.S.A

Interested? Many international students are eligible to work in the U.S. with proper authorization by working at the school or having practical training. Nothing could be better supplementing your study with hands-on training.

About us

StartEdu offers guidance and assistance in placing prospects in desired programs as student visa holders (primarily) into ESL Schools, Colleges and Universities, assistance in extending tourist visa while in the U.S., guidance in changing status from tourist (B1/B2), exchange student (J-1 Cultural Exchange Work and Travel or Au Pair), H1B work visa, agricultural workers H1A to student/tourist visa.

Most applicants qualify to be students in the U.S. There is no age preference or limit, and prospects are not required to be students in their home county. Pay forward, will gladly share our experience, tricks and tips in order to help you succeed in this country.
